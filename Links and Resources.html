GmathIRL: Our official website

GmathIRL helps improve the minds of youths through math courses and competitions held on two discord servers. We've already successfully run one session and the other session is in progress right now. Events such as GAME and Rabbits Hunt are also under planning.
Rabbit's Geometry: The book

The book mainly focuses on AIME and Olympiad level geometry with examples and problem sections. First version has already been published with sketchy Geogebra pictures. To improve its appearance, I'm working on asy and have finished the first 3 chapters thanks to Bratin Dasgupta.
