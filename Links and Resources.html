GmathIRL: Our official website

GmathIRL helps improve the minds of youths through math courses and competitions held on two discord servers. We've already successfully run two sessions (one focuses on Geometry and Calculus, the other helps our students better prepare for AIME) and Rabbits Hunt is currently running in our private server. The GAME competition is also currenly going on!

Rabbit's Geometry: The book

The book mainly focuses on AIME and Olympiad level geometry with examples and problem sections. First version has already been published with sketchy Geogebra pictures. To improve its appearance, I'm working on the asymptote version nd have basically finished 80% chapter graphs thanks to Bratin Dasgupta.
